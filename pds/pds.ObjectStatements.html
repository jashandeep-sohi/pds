insert(index, statement)¶

Insert statement at the given index.

Parameters:
  • statement (Statement) – The statement to insert. It must be an instance of Statement (i.e. Attribute, Group or Object).
  • index (int) – The index at which statement should be inserted. index can be any integer (positive or negative). If it’s negative then it’s taken to mean the index from the end.
